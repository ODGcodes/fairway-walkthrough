    How to use this
    1. Voice Log: Tap "Start Walkthrough" and describe each issue. The assistant will ask for missing details and log it to the spreadsheet. Say "done" when finished.

    2. Photos: Tap "Add Photo." Use 📷 Take Photo for the camera or 🖼️ Gallery to pick an existing photo. Fill in address/building/category, then tap ☁️ Upload.

    3. Review: Tap "Review Entries" to see everything logged to the spreadsheet. Use filters to search by address, category, or reviewer.

    4. Photo Gallery: Tap "View all uploaded photos" inside the photo panel to see, filter, or delete photos.
